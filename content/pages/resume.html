  • Core developer of TOPAS – Monte Carlo simulation software for radiation oncology
  • Wrote an algorithm to sample directions within an elliptical cone and verified its uniformity using a two-level Kolmogorov-Smirnov test
  • Predicted the benefits of proton therapy based upon patient-specific anatomy for skull-base (41 patients), lung (104 pts) and prostate (119 pts) cancer cohorts.
